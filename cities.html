Roseville

Roseville is the most populous city in Placer County, California, located within the Sacramento metropolitan area. As of 2019, the US Census Bureau estimated the city's population to be 141,500.Interstate 80 runs through Roseville and State Route 65 runs through part of the northern edge of the city.

Alexandria

Alexandria was best known for the Lighthouse of Alexandria,one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World; its Great Library.It was at one time the largest city in the ancient world before being eventually overtaken by Rome.

Ahmedabad

Ahmedabad has emerged as an important economic and industrial hub in India. It is the second-largest producer of cotton in India, due to which it was known as the 'Manchester of India' along with Kanpur. Ahmedabad's stock exchange (before it was shut down in 2018) was the country's second oldest.
